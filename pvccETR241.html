Nation Weather Service Station

I found the NWSS frequency for Albemarle County, which is 162.450. I had to play around with the bandwidth and the PPM, which I set at 18. Once I made those couple of adjustments, I was able to hear our local station fairly clear. It was nice to have the visual display of the sound waves, because I could see the signal was present and was a notion to do some fine tuning.
